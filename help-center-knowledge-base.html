Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about the University Residence

How do I apply for residence?

To apply for residence, visit the housing portal on the university's website and complete the online application form. Ensure you have all required documents ready.

What are the accommodation fees?

Accommodation fees vary based on the type of room and amenities provided. You can find a detailed fee structure on the university's residence management webpage.

Can I change my room after allocation?

Room changes are subject to availability and approval by the residence management. Submit a request through the housing portal for consideration.

What facilities are available in the residence?

  • Each residence hall includes Wi-Fi, laundry rooms, common areas, and study rooms.
  • Some halls also have fitness centers and recreational facilities.

How do I report maintenance issues?

  • Log into the housing portal and fill out a maintenance request form.
  • Emergency issues should be reported directly to the residence management office.

Can I host guests in my room?

Guests are allowed with prior approval from the residence management. Guests must adhere to all residence policies during their stay.

Are there any curfews?

There are no curfews, but residents are expected to maintain quiet hours from 10 PM to 7 AM to ensure a conducive study environment for all.

Can I request a roommate?

Yes, roommate requests can be made during the application process. Both parties must mutually agree to the request for it to be considered.

What happens if I lose my room key?

  • Report the loss immediately to the residence management office.
  • A replacement fee will be charged for issuing a new key.

What safety measures are in place?

  • The residence has 24/7 security personnel and surveillance cameras.
  • Residents are issued access cards for secure entry to the buildings.
